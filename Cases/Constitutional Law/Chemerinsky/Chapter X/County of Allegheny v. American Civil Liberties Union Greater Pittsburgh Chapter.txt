Citation
492 U.S. 573, 109 S. Ct. 3086, 106 L. Ed. 2d 472, 1989 U.S.

Brief Fact Summary
Religious holiday decorations were displayed on some of the city’s major governmental buildings.

Synopsis of Rule of Law
Government may not endorse a particular religion. Endorsement is determined by the context of the use of religious symbols.
