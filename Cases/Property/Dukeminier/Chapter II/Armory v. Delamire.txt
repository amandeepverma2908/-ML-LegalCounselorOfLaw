Citation
93 ER 664, Volume 93

Brief Fact Summary
<br /><span class='heading'>Synopsis of Rule of Law.</span><br />

Facts
Armory (P), a chimney sweep’s boy, found a jewel and took it to the Delamire (D), a goldsmith, to know what it was. After D’s apprentice removed the stone, D offered to buy the jewel. When P refused and asked for its return, D returned it without the stones.

Issue
Whether a finder of lost or abandoned property has a superior property right against all others except the rightful owner?

Held
Yes.

Discussion
The first finder of lost or abandoned property has superior property rights in such property against all others except the true owner.
