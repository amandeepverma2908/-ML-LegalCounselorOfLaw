Synopsis of Rule of Law
When the intent of the parties to a deed or conveyance, where the grantor conveys a fee, is clear and convincing as to their intent to create a joint tenancy, then a joint tenancy is created, and the right of survivorship is not to be considered a contingent remainder.

Issue
What is the correct status of the parties as to the property?

Held
The Plaintiff is the owner in fee. Appeal allowed and case remanded with instructions.

Discussion
This case provides a good discussion of the joint tenancy, which is an essential concept in property law.
